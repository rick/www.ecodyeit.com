Natural dyework for your sampling and production needs

Artisan Natural Dyeworks is an artisanal dyehouse based in Nashville, Tennessee, specializing in the use of all-natural plant- and earth-based dyes to dye garments, piece goods and production yardage. As one of the only U.S. based companies to provide natural dye services at a scale suitable for independent designers, we collaborate with nature and you to create ecologically-friendly colors for your fashion line. We grow and gather our own dye materials, work with farmers to cultivate natural dye plants on a larger scale, and design and use minimal-waste systems to create distinctive colors for your clothing lines.

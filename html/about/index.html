Our eco-dye service is handcrafted to suit our clients

  • Artisan-quality garment, piece, and yardage dyeing using all-natural dyes is our specialty.
  • We are available for custom color consultation, development, and matching.
  • We do sample dyeing and production runs with equal ease.
  • Our services are available to small-run clients, but we are scaled to handle large runs as well.
  • We have travelled and researched extensively to refine and customize traditional natural dyeing techniques, making them suitable for contemporary needs and clients.


Our eco-dye service is sustainable.


  • We use plant- and earth-based dyes, many grown locally, for all our colors.
  • Our production processes are low impact.
  • We use minimal water, no toxic additives, and are developing a “zero-waste” operating model for our production facility.
  • Through our natural dye research farm and regional farmer collective, we sustainably cultivate the raw materials for our colors.
  • For many of our colorways, “farm to fashion” color traceability is available.


Our eco-dye service is beautiful and distinctive.


  • Since they are developed from natural sources, our colors are harmonious.
  • Subtle tonal differences are aesthetically appealing and provide differentiation in the retail sector.
  • Timeless colors provide season-to-season versatility and longevity.


Our eco-dye service is transparent and traceable.


  • All work is done at our production facility in Nashville, TN.
  • We provide documentation of our processes and materials.
  • Our "field and factory" are available for marketing documentation for designers interested in communicating story of how pieces are made.


We offer additional custom services.


  • Custom natural dye printing available - one of the only providers of this service in the United States.
  • Our "best buy" fabrics and colorways utilize "wastage" dyebaths, providing beautiful tones at a great value
  • Our dye service is available on all natural fibers, as well as on select synthetics.
  • We offer re-dye services to help designers reclaim old lines or fabrics.
